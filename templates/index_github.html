Actionable Insights

Low-Value Customers

Customers at risk of churning. Focus on re-engagement.

  • Implement targeted email campaigns with special offers.
  • Conduct surveys to understand and address their pain points.
  • Use past purchase history for personalized recommendations.
Medium-Value Customers

Loyal customers with growth potential. Focus on up-selling.

  • Introduce loyalty programs to reward their engagement.
  • Recommend complementary or higher-value products.
  • Offer exclusive content or early access to new items.
High-Value Customers

Your most valuable customers. Focus on retention and advocacy.

  • Provide VIP treatment with exclusive benefits and dedicated support.
  • Involve them in product feedback and co-creation sessions.
  • Encourage referrals through an attractive incentive program.
